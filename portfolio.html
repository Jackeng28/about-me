Scratch Project

This is a Racing Minion Game where the objective of the game is to finish the race before the Evil Minion does. You get to choose between three minions then race against the evil one. You will get a three second count down then begin. You move with the arrow keys and if you go off the track you will slow down until you are back on it. Whoever does three laps first wins, and the respected screen is shown.
